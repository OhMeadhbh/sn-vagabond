Hello. Welcome to the node.js development appliance by Smithee, Spelvin, Agnew & Plinge. This web application is being run from a virtual machine with a pre-configured node.js installation. It gives you a consistent development environment to quickly develop your own node.js applications. For more information about configuring this appliance, look at the Online Help.
